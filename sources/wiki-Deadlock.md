**Deadlock** commonly refers to:

 
- [Deadlock (computer science)](./Deadlock_(computer_science)), a situation where two or more processes are each waiting for the other to take action
- [Deadlock (locksmithing)](./Deadlock_(locksmithing)) or deadbolt, a physical door locking mechanism
- [Political deadlock](./Political_deadlock) or gridlock, a situation of difficulty passing laws that satisfy the needs of the people
- [Negotiation deadlock](./Negotiation_deadlock) or an impasse, a situation where two sides bargaining can't reach an agreement
- [Tie (draw)](./Tie_(draw)), in competitive sports, identical or inconclusive results
