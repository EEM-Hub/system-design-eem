**Etag** can refer to:

- [etags](./Ctags#Etags), the ctags utility that comes with Emacs
- [NERC Tag](./NERC_Tag), also known as E-Tag, a transaction on the North American bulk electricity market
- [e-TAG](./E-TAG), an electronic tolling system used on tollways in Australia
- [European Technology Assessment Group](./European_Technology_Assessment_Group), a network of scientific institutions
