**Fan-out** has multiple meanings in [software engineering](./Software_engineering).

 

## Message-oriented middleware

 

In [message-oriented middleware](./Message-oriented_middleware) solutions, **fan-out** is a [messaging pattern](./Messaging_pattern) used to model an [information exchange](./Information_exchange) that implies the delivery (or spreading) of a [message](./Message) to one or multiple [destinations](./Message_queue) possibly in parallel, and not halting the process that executes the messaging to wait for any response to that message.[[1]](./Fan-out_(software)#cite_note-1)[[2]](./Fan-out_(software)#cite_note-2)[[3]](./Fan-out_(software)#cite_note-3)

 

## Software design and quality assurance

 

In [software construction](./Software_construction), the fan-out of a class or method is the number of other classes used by that class or the number of other methods called by that method.[[4]](./Fan-out_(software)#cite_note-4)

 

Additionally, fan-out has impact on the quality of a software.[[5]](./Fan-out_(software)#cite_note-5)[[6]](./Fan-out_(software)#cite_note-6)[[7]](./Fan-out_(software)#cite_note-7)
